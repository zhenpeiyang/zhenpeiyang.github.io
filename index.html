Short Bio

I am a Research Scientist at Waymo. I received my PhD degree in 2022 from the Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin, where I was advised by Prof. Qixing Huang. I received my B.Eng. degree from the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University in 2016. I was an Apple Scholar in the area of Computer Vision and Augmented Reality.
